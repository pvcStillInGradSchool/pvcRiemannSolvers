Detailed Description

template<std::floating_point Scalar, int Q, int kAlpha, int kBeta>
struct mini::integrator::Jacobi< Scalar, Q, kAlpha, kBeta >

Gauss–Jacobi quadrature rules, i.e. \( \int_{-1}^{1} f(\xi) (1-\xi)^\alpha (1+\xi)^\beta \,\mathrm{d}\xi \approx \sum_{q=1}^{Q} w_q f(\xi_q) \).

Template Parameters
ScalarType of scalar variables.
QNnumber of quadrature points, which must be chosen from { 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 }.
kAlphaPower of \( (1-\xi) \) in the integrand.
kBetaPower of \( (1+\xi) \) in the integrand.
